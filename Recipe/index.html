Chocolate Chip Cookies

a classic American treat, beloved for their warm, gooey centers and crisp edges. Made with a simple mixture of flour, butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla, they feature semi-sweet chocolate chips that melt beautifully during baking. The contrast of sweet dough and rich chocolate creates a delightful flavor profile, making them a favorite for cookies lovers of all ages. Perfect with a glass of milk or enjoyed on their own, chocolate chip cookies are a timeless comfort food.
